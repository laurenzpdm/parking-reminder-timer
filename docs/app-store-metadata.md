Description:

Parking Reminder Timer helps you avoid stressful meter surprises. Start a parking session in seconds, save a note about your spot, get clear time-left reminders, and find your way back to the car faster.

The app is built for real parking moments, not complicated planning. Open it when you park, choose the time on the meter, add a quick note, and let the friendly meter companion keep watch.
