Parking Reminder Timer helps you avoid stressful meter surprises. Start a parking session in seconds, save a note about your spot, get clear time-left reminders, and find your way back to the car faster.

The app is built for real parking moments, not complicated planning. Open it when you park, choose the time on the meter, add a quick note, and let the friendly meter companion keep watch.

Free features:
- Quick parking timer
- Spot note and location label
- Ticket-free streak tracking
- Shareable streak moment

Pro features:
- Unlimited parking sessions
- Smart pre-expiry reminder presets
- Rich ticket-free streak cards
- Annual plan with a 7-day free trial

Parking Reminder Timer does not pay for parking, reserve parking, or guarantee that users avoid fines. It helps users save their own timer and reminder information.
