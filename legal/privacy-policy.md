# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 16, 2026

Parking Reminder Timer is provided by Thümmler AI GmbH. Contact: support@nachhilfe-mentor.de, phone 015758249014.

The app stores parking sessions, timer settings, ticket-free streaks, notes, and optional location labels on the user's device. If the user grants location permission, the app may use approximate current location to help label or find the parked car. Location data is used for the app experience and is not sold.

Subscriptions are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. RevenueCat may process purchase identifiers, subscription status, device identifiers, and diagnostic information needed to provide subscription access.

The app may use local notifications to remind users before a parking timer expires. The app does not provide parking payment, parking reservation, legal, financial, or safety guarantees.

Users can request support or deletion guidance by contacting support@nachhilfe-mentor.de.
